Support

Help with Clasidocs — the local study-notes app for students.

Need help or found a bug? Email contact@clasidocs.com. We usually reply within a few days.

When you email, please include

  • Your device model and iOS version (Settings → General → About).
  • The Clasidocs version (App Store → your apps).
  • What you expected to happen, and what happened instead.
  • Steps to reproduce, and a screenshot if you can.

Common questions

Where is my data stored?
Everything you create stays on your device. There is no account and no cloud sync, so your notes are not backed up by Clasidocs — use your device's normal backup if you want a copy.

Does Clasidocs use the internet?
Only to show a preview image when you save a web link (e.g. a YouTube thumbnail). Nothing else is sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I move my notes to a new phone?
Use a subject's Export in Settings to save a ZIP, then import it on the new device.
